Popular Workouts

HIIT: High-Intensity Interval Training

High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) involves short bursts of intense exercise followed by brief recovery periods. It's a time-efficient workout that boosts metabolism and burns calories effectively.

  • Duration: 20-30 minutes
  • Benefits: Fat burning, cardiovascular health
  • Intensity: High

Pilates for Core Strength

Pilates focuses on core strength, flexibility, and body awareness. It involves controlled movements and breathing techniques to improve posture and muscle tone.

  • Duration: 45-60 minutes
  • Benefits: Core strength, flexibility
  • Intensity: Medium
